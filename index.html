"But, I don't know you people"

Yeah, I know. And I'm trying to change that.

My name is Hashim Warren. My father, George Warren came to New York in the late 70s with a huge wave of other striving Jamaicans. Some of those immigrants included his brother Denzil, and his cousins Harvey and Tony.
